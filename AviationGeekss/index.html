Airbus S.A.S, an European Aircraft Manufacturer that established itself as the world's second largest marker of commercial aircraft. Airbus was formally formed in 1970 by French and German Aerospace firms. The main Aim of the the Company was to compete with the long-established American Manufacturer of militarty and commercial aircraft 'Boeing', by launching short- to medium-range, high capacity jetliners. Airbus launched its first commercial jetliner, the A300, which entered into service in 1974. Airbus later became increasingly interested in developing and selling of military based Aircraft in the late 1990s and established a foot into the Military Aviation Market marking a huge blow to it's American competitor.

Boeing is amongs the largest airplane manufacturer and continous the biggest and the most advanced airplanes till date. William Edward an American pioneer founded Aero Products Company in 1916 which a year later was renamed to the Boeing Company. The company developed it's first B&W Seaplane which was given the name Bluebill. Later Boeing developed a similar Seaplane called Model C which was later led to the company's first financial success into the market of United states. Boeing started it's mark in the Aviation market by designing and developing military aircrafts for United states during and later of the First World War. In 1928 Boeing designed it's first American commercial based airliner specificially for passenger comfort and convenience called Model 80. The first Boeing aircraft to carry 12 people onboard.
